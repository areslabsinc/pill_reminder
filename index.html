Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22 June 2025

Introduction

Pill Reminder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or medical data. All information you enter remains solely on your device.

What Data Do We Collect?

None. We do not collect or transmit any personal data, health data, or usage data. All app activity and data are confined to your mobile device.

Data Storage and Security

  • All medication and reminder data is stored locally on your device.
  • The app uses Apple’s Core Data system with iOS encryption for data-at-rest protection.
  • If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently erased.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If enabled, your data may be synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data, and it is managed solely by Apple under your control.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. Your information is never stored on our servers — in fact, we don't even have servers.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Your Privacy Rights

  • You have full control over your data through your device.
  • You can delete data at any time by removing the app.
  • We do not store any data, so there is nothing for us to provide, delete, or manage on your behalf.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this policy and the app accordingly.
